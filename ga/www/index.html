Welcome to the OpenOffice.org Irish localisation project

We're a small group of volunteers committed to providing free software in the Irish language. If you would like to help with translating, proofreading, testing, or other aspects of the project, please write to us at dev@ga.openoffice.org. The translation of OpenOffice.org 2.0 is more than 80 percent complete.

OpenOffice.org is an open source and open standards complete productivity suite compatible with all major office suites. It's available for free and has been downloaded by an estimated 16 million+ people! So what does the suite include?

  • Writer: a powerful tool for creating professional documents, reports, newsletters, and brochures
  • Calc: a feature-packed spreadsheet
  • Impress: the fastest, most powerful way to create effective multimedia presentations
  • Draw: will produce everything from simple diagrams to dynamic 3D illustrations and special effects
  • Base: the tools you need for day to day database work in a simple spreadsheet-like form
  • a component for mathematical equations
